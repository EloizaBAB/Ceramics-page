What is ceramics?

One of my favourite hobbies

The word "ceramic" comes from the Greek word meaning "pottery". Traditional ceramics are clay based but there are other categories of pottery such as earthenware, stoneware and porcelain. The composition of the clays used, type of additives and firing temperatures determine the nature of the end product. The freedom of creativity it gives us is what amazes me, I just have to think and then try to create whatever it is that I desire, and that, you cannot find in any hobby!
